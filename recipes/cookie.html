Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookie, just the alliteration is great on its own. But the taste, the iconic contrast between chocolate chip and cookie dough, is what firmly establishes this as a favorite to children, fuzzy blue puppets, and snack-enjoyers all over the world. This recipe has been perfectly crafted to bring this classic to new heights. And if you take a bite out of one, it kind of looks like the moon...

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
    • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned
